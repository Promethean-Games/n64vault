Let's keep it real - emulators are 100% legal. They're just software that mimics hardware. What matters is how you use them.

What's totally fine

About Netcade

Netcade is a free, open-source web-based Nintendo 64 emulator that lets you relive the golden era of gaming directly in your browser.

Our Mission

We believe classic games should be preserved and accessible. Netcade provides a legal way to play your personal game backups without installing software or hunting for sketchy downloads.

Features

  • Play N64 games directly in your browser
  • Full keyboard and gamepad support with customizable controls
  • Save states to pick up where you left off
  • Multiplayer support for up to 4 players via WebRTC
  • Works on desktop and mobile devices
  • Progressive Web App - install it on your home screen

Technology

Netcade is powered by EmulatorJS, an open-source emulator framework. The emulator runs entirely in your browser using WebAssembly for near-native performance.

Open Source

Netcade is open source and community-driven. We welcome contributions, bug reports, and feature suggestions.
